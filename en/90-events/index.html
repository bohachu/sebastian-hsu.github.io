  • Jan. 2018

    Design Competition for the 90th Anniversary Celebration Slogan and Logo

    To celebrate its 90th anniversary, Academia Sinica held a design competition for the most representative slogan and logo. The winning slogan submission not only pays tribute the anniversary celebrations but also showcases the essence of research—a perennial pursuit of the truth with a firm, steady, and resolute attitude. The winning logo design fuses the concept of globalization with elements from the three key research areas that make up Academia Sinica—mathematics and physical sciences, life sciences, and the humanities and social sciences. At the same time, it also simultaneously upholds the research philosophy of Academia Sinica: to achieve sustainable development and create a better future for humanity.

  • August 25, 2018

    Island-wide Popular Science Lecture Series in Tainan

    Organized by Academia Sinica, the Island-wide Popular Science Lecture Series will be held in cities across Taiwan to bring research closer to the people. The Tainan lecture will be held at the National Museum of Taiwan Literature on Saturday, August 25, 2018. Besides featuring distinguished speakers, an Augmented Reality (AR) experience zone will be set up at the event for the public to experience for themselves, first hand, what it’s like to be a researcher at Academia Sinica and to better understand different fields of research. This special lecture series offers a unique opportunity for residents residing outside of Taipei to learn more about the goals and research achievements of Academia Sinica.

  • October 18, 2018

    The Academia Sinica Lecture

    The Academia Sinica Lecture, established in 2009, is a prize lecture of Academia Sinica and represents the highest honor in this Academy. The Lecture is to recognize an individual who has made exceptional contribution to research and who has the ability to communicate the knowledge and wisdom of his or her research in a broadly appealing way. It is expected that the Lectureship will bring to this Academy not only new knowledge but also new spirit.

  • October 27, 2018

    Academia Sinica Open House

    Seeking to bring the world of research closer to the people, beginning in 1998, Academia Sinica holds an Open House every year. Over the years, this event has been well received by the public. Activities organized for the Open House are all carefully designed by researchers from the various institutes and centers across Academia Sinica. Through a professional yet easy-to-understand as well as fun and interactive manner, researchers share new and cutting edge research findings in fields spanning from the humanities to the hard sciences and social sciences with the public.

  • November 1-2, 2018

    K.T. Wang Bioorganic Chemistry Lectureship

    In order to honor Dr. Wang and carry over the mission to nourish the Bioorganic Chemistry Research in Taiwan, a group of the Taiwanese scientists including Dr. Y.T. Lee and Dr. C.H. Wong organized and helped the founding of “K-T Wang Bioorganic Chemistry Educational Foundation” in October 2000. The K-T Wang foundation enables more students and young scholars to have the opportunity to meet with world-renowned scientists face-to-face. Once a year the foundation awards a world-famous scholar to give talks on his/her research experiences. The purpose is to inspire the youth in this field and thus speed up the progress of Bioorganic Chemistry research in Taiwan.
